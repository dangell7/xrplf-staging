A5 — Internal Status Report

XRPL SDK & Wallet ecosystem health

Prepared by Denis Angell, Director of Technology Date 20 May 2026 Audience XRPL Foundation Director (internal) Asana A5 task
Conflict-of-interest disclosure. Three SDKs in §SDKs (XrplCSharp, xrpl.csharp.unity, XRPLSwift) are maintained by the author through Transia-RnD. Funding, takeover, or archive decisions on these repos must be made by Foundation staff not involved in their maintenance.

Executive summary

The XRPL client-side stack is broader and more active than 18 months ago — but the surface is still dangerously concentrated. Two SDKs (xrpl.js, xrpl-py) are credibly tracking the 2025–26 amendment set. Most of the others are bus-factor-1, lagging on amendment coverage, or both.
